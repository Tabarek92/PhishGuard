Common Red Flags
  • Urgent or threatening language demanding immediate action
  • Sender domain doesn't match the claimed organization
  • Requests for passwords, SSN, or financial credentials
  • Suspicious or mismatched hyperlinks and URLs
  • Poor grammar, spelling errors, or unnatural phrasing
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers — prizes, refunds, windfalls
  • Unexpected attachments or requests to enable macros
